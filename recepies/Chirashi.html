Deconstructed Sushi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of sushi rice
  • 4 cups of cold water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • ½ cup of rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 800 grams of fresh salmon, cut into medium-sized cubes
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of mirin
  • 1 teaspoon of sriracha (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • 1 tablespoon of finely chopped green onions
  • 10 small and very fresh cucumbers
  • ½ thinly sliced purple onion
  • ½ cup dried wakame seaweed
  • Juice from half a lemon
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice well in running water for about 3-4 minutes.
  2. Transfer rice to a pot with water and salt, bring to a boil.
  3. Lower the heat, cover, and cook for 12 minutes.
  4. Turn off the heat and leave the pot covered for another 15 minutes.
  5. Mix sushi sauce (rice vinegar, sugar, salt) and pour over cooked rice, then mix.
  6. For the salmon marinade, combine soy sauce, mayonnaise, mirin, sriracha, sesame oil, lemon juice, and green onions.
  7. Cut cucumbers lengthwise, scrape the inside, and slice. Soak wakame seaweed in cold water, then mix cucumbers with seaweed and onion. Season with soy, mirin, lemon, and sesame oil.
  8. Assemble the dish: place a cup of prepared rice in a bowl, add salmon with sauce, cucumber and seaweed salad, and additional toppings as desired.
  9. Top with fresh coriander leaves, chopped green onion, crispy garlic, black sesame, and other preferred toppings.
