Chiken Skewers Satay

Description

Sata is a classic Thai dish of chicken skewers marinated and grilled. All over Thailand you can find someone grilling satay on every street corner. The dish is usually served with a sauce and pickled cucumbers on the side.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 150 ml canola oil
  • 100 ml soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of grated palm sugar
  • 1/2 kilo chicken breast cut into strips

Instructions

  1. Mix together all the ingredients except the chicken in a large bowl.
  2. Add the chicken and mix well until the whole chicken is covered in the marinade. Place in the refrigerator overnight or for at least 4 hours.
  3. Skewer the chicken strips.
  4. Place the skewers on the grill or a hot grill pan and grill for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  5. Serve hot with peanut sauce and pickled cucumber salad on the side.
