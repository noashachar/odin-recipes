Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of plain flour (280 g)
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon of baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon fine salt
  • 220 g of soft butter
  • 1 cup light demerara sugar or white sugar
  • ¼ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract or puree
  • 1 egg (L)
  • 100 grams of chopped dark chocolate

Preparation

  1. In a medium bowl, mix the dry ingredients - flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a mixer with a guitar hook, mix butter and sugars well for 2-3 minutes until sugar joins the butter and melts a little.
  3. Clean the sides of the bowl and add an egg. Turn on the mixer again until the egg is incorporated. Add the vanilla and mix.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the mixture and mix just until everything is incorporated.
  5. Add the dark chocolate, run the mixer on high speed for 3 seconds.
  6. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and transfer the mass to the refrigerator for two hours.
  7. After two hours, heat the oven to 200 degrees, line a pan with baking paper.
  8. Use your hands to form balls of the desired size (large cookies: 100 grams, small cookies: ping pong ball).
  9. Arrange the cookies in a mold with plenty of spaces.
  10. Transfer the mold to the freezer for a few minutes.
  11. Heat the oven to 200 degrees.
  12. Arrange the cookies in a mold with plenty of spaces.
  13. Transfer the mold to the freezer for a few minutes.
  14. Bake for 15-20 minutes until edges are browned. Adjust based on personal preference.
  15. Remove from the oven and transfer to a cooling rack for the final texture.
